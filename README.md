## Key Concepts

- **Creator Mode** — an AI that designs, spawns, observes, and iterates on AI civilisations
- **Directed mode** — systematic organisational search for optimal task configurations
- **Emergent mode** — exploration of conditions that maximise emergence
- **Self-referential CMI** — the field exploring itself through the creation of AI collectives

## Related Fields

- **Collective Machine Intelligence (CMI)** — coined in Paper 4
- **Computational Organisational Theory (COT)** — the methodological subfield of CMI
